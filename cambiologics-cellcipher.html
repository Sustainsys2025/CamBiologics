CellCipher

Decoding Cellular Therapy Through AI

CellCipher represents a revolutionary approach to cellular therapy, deploying advanced deep learning to decode the complex language of living cells. Cell and gene therapies hold immense promise, yet their development is hampered by cellular heterogeneity, unpredictable behavior, and limited understanding of therapeutic mechanisms.

Our platform integrates single-cell transcriptomics, phenotypic profiling, and dynamic imaging to create comprehensive cellular fingerprints. By learning patterns across millions of cellular states, CellCipher predicts therapeutic efficacy, identifies optimal cell populations, and guides precision engineering of next-generation cell therapies with unprecedented accuracy.
